SpeedSpree

Unity 6 high-speed roguelike where procedural level generation is tuned for flow, readability, and replayable speedrun-level mastery.

  • Unity 6
  • 3D Roguelike
  • Procedural Levels
  • Momentum Movement

Procedural Pipeline

SpeedSpree builds levels from modular high-speed chunks selected by weighted rules. The generator prioritizes momentum continuity so the player can stay in flow without random layouts creating unfair dead-ends.

Generation Rules

  • Chunk stitching enforces entry/exit compatibility to keep route continuity at high velocity.
  • Validation passes check spacing, timing windows, and traversal feasibility before a segment is accepted.
  • Hazard placement uses weighted pools to scale challenge while preserving visual readability.
  • Seed-based generation supports deterministic replay for balancing, QA, and speedrun sharing.

Design Intent

  • Generate surprising layouts that still reward precision and route planning.
  • Reduce frustration by filtering impossible or low-readability segment combinations.
  • Support rapid iteration by tuning generator weights rather than hand-authoring every level.
